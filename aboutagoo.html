ABOUT AGOO

Brief History

Agoo, located in the province of La Union, is one of the oldest settlements in the region. Long before Spanish colonization, it was already a thriving trading port where Chinese, Japanese, and Malay merchants exchanged goods. Over time, it grew into a progressive town while preserving its rich cultural heritage.

Cultural Heritage

Agoo takes pride in its vibrant traditions, festivals, and historical landmarks. Its heritage reflects a blend of indigenous and foreign influences, seen in its architecture, cuisine, and community celebrations.

Mission & Vision

Vision

To be a leading tourist destination that celebrates culture, preserves nature, and promotes sustainable development.

Mission

To promote Agoo's beauty, history, and traditions while creating opportunities for the local community and protecting the environment.

Local Identity & Values

The people of Agoo are known for their hospitality, resilience, and strong sense of community. Visitors can expect a warm welcome and an authentic Filipino experience.
