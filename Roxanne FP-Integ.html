Agoo, La Union

Located along the scenic shores of the South China Sea, Agoo is one of the oldest and most historic towns in La Union. Known for its rich cultural heritage, warm hospitality, and beautiful natural spots, it offers a perfect mix of history, adventure, and relaxation for every traveler.

Must-Visit Attractions

Basilica Minore of Our Lady of Charity

One of the oldest and most beautiful churches in the region, known for its stunning architecture and religious significance.

Agoo Dam & Eco Park

A perfect spot for nature lovers, offering fresh air, scenic views, and relaxing activities perfect for families.

Agoo Beaches

Clean and peaceful shorelines ideal for swimming, sunset viewing, and enjoying the cool sea breeze.
